1. Introduction This Privacy Policy explains what information DevBot ("the Bot") collects, how it is used, and how it is protected. By inviting or using DevBot, server owners and users acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy. 2. Information We Collect DevBot only stores information necessary to provide its features. Depending on which features a server uses, the Bot may store: Discord Server (Guild) IDs Discord Channel IDs Discord Role IDs Discord User IDs Twitch channel names configured by server administrators Moderation settings Ticket configuration Warning counts Whitelisted or banned words Server-specific bot configuration Changelog channel settings Other configuration explicitly created by server administrators DevBot does not collect passwords, payment information, private messages, or any personal information outside what Discord already makes available to bots. 3. How Information Is Used Stored information is used solely to provide the Bot's features, including but not limited to: Moderation Ticket management Twitch live notifications Logging Warning systems Server customization Slash command functionality Information is never used for advertising or profiling users. 4. Data Sharing DevBot does not sell, rent, or share stored information with third parties except: when required by applicable law; when necessary to comply with a legal obligation; when necessary to protect the integrity or security of the Bot or its services. 5. Data Retention Configuration data remains stored only as long as it is reasonably necessary for the Bot to provide its services. If the Bot is removed from a server, associated configuration data may be deleted after a reasonable period. 6. Security Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security. 7. User Rights Server owners may request deletion of their server's stored configuration data after removing the Bot from their server. Requests may be submitted using the contact information provided below. 8. Third-Party Services DevBot may communicate with third-party services that are necessary for its functionality, such as: Discord Twitch These services operate under their own privacy policies. 9. Children's Privacy DevBot is not directed toward children under the age required to use Discord under its Terms of Service. 10. Changes to this Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of DevBot after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. 11. Contact Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to the bot developer through the official support server or other contact methods made available by the developer.
